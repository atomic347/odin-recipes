2-Ingredient Pineapple Whip

This is another 2-Ingredient Pineapple Whip and its pretty self-explanatory.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup fresh frozen pineapple
  • 1 cup coconut-pineapple sparkling water

STEPS

  1. Combine frozen pineapple and sparkling water in a blender; blend until icy and frothy, 1 to 2 minutes.
